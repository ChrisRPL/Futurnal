# Future Vision & Strategic Roadmap

## Personalization Through Federated Learning
- Develop an opt-in federated learning program that allows aggregate model updates without sharing raw user data.
- Implement encrypted gradient aggregation so the central service never receives identifiable content.
- Focus on improving entity extraction accuracy, insight relevance, and conversational guidance while preserving privacy.
- Communicate benefits clearly to drive participation and create a virtuous network effect.

## Decentralized Knowledge Commons
- Offer a voluntary pathway for users to contribute anonymized, structured PKG derivatives to a shared research dataset.
- Explore decentralized protocols or cooperative governance models to maintain user control over contributions.
- Enable novel research into cognitive patterns, creativity, and productivity while adhering to strict privacy guarantees.

## Movement-Level Impact
- Position Futurnal as a counter-narrative to centralized data platforms by demonstrating ethical, user-centric intelligence.
- Foster a community of practitioners, researchers, and users iterating on introspective AI methodologies.
- Leverage content marketing and thought leadership to shape discourse around personal causal intelligence.

## Strategic Milestones Beyond MVP
- Launch federated learning pilot once user base surpasses critical mass and infrastructure is production-ready.
- Expand data source connectors to cover calendars, messaging, and quantified-self devices.
- Introduce API access for third-party plugins to enhance analysis while respecting data sovereignty.
- Pursue partnerships with academic institutions for privacy-preserving introspection research.
