# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a harassment-free, inclusive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional context.

## Scope

This applies in project spaces, issue trackers, pull requests, and community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban users for violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents to project maintainers through private contact. Include links, timestamps, and context when possible.
