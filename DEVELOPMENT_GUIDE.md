# Futurnal Development Guide

## How to Use This Guide
Follow the sequence below to onboard quickly, understand the product vision, and jump into execution. Each referenced file begins with its own summary for easy scanning.

## Step 1 — Understand the Why
1. `docs/product-vision.md` — Defines mission, target users, brand pillars, and the phased journey from Archivist to Guide.
2. `docs/market-analysis.md` — Breaks down the competitive landscape and articulates the prosumer opportunity.
3. `docs/key-difference.md` — Clarifies how Futurnal extends beyond standard GraphRAG into causal introspection.

## Step 2 — Internalize the Future Direction
4. `docs/future-vision.md` — Outlines long-term strategies such as federated learning and decentralized knowledge commons.

## Step 3 — Dive into the Technical Blueprint
5. `architecture/system-architecture.md` — Describes the layered technical design spanning on-device inference, PKG construction, and causal insight workflows.

## Step 4 — Confirm Requirements & Roadmap
6. `requirements/system-requirements.md` — Lists detailed functional and non-functional requirements aligned to user goals.
7. `requirements/roadmap.md` — Provides the phased implementation timeline, milestones, KPIs, and risk mitigation.

## Step 5 — Execute by Development Phase
8. `prompts/phase-1-archivist.md` — Ready-to-use prompts for building data ingestion, PKG foundation, and hybrid search.
9. `prompts/phase-2-analyst.md` — Prompts guiding Emergent Insights, ranking heuristics, and notification systems.
10. `prompts/phase-3-guide.md` — Prompts covering conversational causal exploration and Aspirational Self integration.
11. `docs/phase-1/local-files-production-plan/01-integration-performance-tests.md` — Runbook for Local Files Connector integration/performance suite, including fixture generation, CI workflows, and telemetry benchmarks.

## Additional Tips
- Revisit the prompts as checklists during implementation sprints.
- Use the requirements documents to validate acceptance criteria before shipping features.
- Update summaries as documents evolve to keep cross-references accurate.
